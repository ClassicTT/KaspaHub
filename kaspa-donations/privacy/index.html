Kaspa Donations Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2025

Kaspa Donations is a browser extension designed to help users discover and use Kaspa donation addresses on websites.
This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Kaspa Donations extension respects your data - it does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect or track your IP address, browsing history, or any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not send any data to external servers.
  • We do not use cookies, analytics, or trackers.

2. How the Extension Works

Kaspa Donations operates entirely within your browser. When activated, it scans the current page for specific <meta> tags (kaspa:wallet and kaspa:title) to display donation details. All processing happens locally on your device.

3. Permissions

The extension requests minimal permissions:

  • activeTab - so it can read the current page only when you activate it.
  • scripting - to inject a script that reads meta tags on the page.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if necessary. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective date."

5. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at https://x.com/Kaspahuborg.
